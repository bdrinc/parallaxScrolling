PICO Lamp by AEC-Commerce
The Pico Lamp is a multifunctional stationary or mobile lighting device that is specially designed to meet the needs of rural households and recreational activities.
Charging is highly flexible, it can be powered by solar modules, batteries, or AC adapters. The integrated Phocos MPPT charge controller extends battery lifespan and provides light for up to 55 hours.It can even be used to charge other devices
Charging
Some additional features include:
  • Soft touch on/off swich
  • Three adjustable light levels for best comfort
  • Glow in the dark band which makes it easy to find when dark
  • Automatic reduction of the lighting level when batteries are running down
